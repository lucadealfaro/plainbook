# Privacy Policy

This app is designed to run on your local machine (laptop or desktop). It uses cloud services for generating code and for verifying code. 
To access such services, you have to either provide API keys, or log in with your Google account for Gemini. 

The information that is sent to the AI cloud services may comprise any content of the notebook, including text, code, variable content, and execution results.  If you wish to see the information that is sent to the cloud services, include `--dump-ai-requests` among the command-line parameters.
